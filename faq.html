Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to rent a car?

To rent a car, you'll need a valid driver's license, a credit card for the security deposit, and to meet our age requirements (usually 21 years or older).

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before the pickup time without any fees. For changes within 24 hours of pickup, additional charges may apply.

Is insurance included in the rental price?

Basic insurance is included in the rental price. However, we offer additional coverage options for your peace of mind. You can choose these when making your reservation or at the time of pickup.

What is your fuel policy?

We have a "full-to-full" fuel policy. This means you'll receive the car with a full tank and are expected to return it with a full tank. If the car is returned with less fuel, you'll be charged for the difference.

Do you offer one-way rentals?

Yes, we offer one-way rentals between many of our locations. Additional fees may apply, and availability may be limited. Please contact us for specific one-way rental queries.
